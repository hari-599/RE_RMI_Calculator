ABOUT THE TOOL

The Renewable Energy â€“ Regional Maturity Index (RE-RMI) is a strategic assessment tool designed to evaluate the progress of renewable energy transition within the domestic sector of a region. RE-RMI consolidates the governance, local market conditions and consumer perception in a region, and identify the critical indicators that would help in expedite household energy transition.

This tool offers a clear snapshot of a region's current maturity level in terms of renewable energy transition, supporting evidence-based decision-making by local self-governments or Implementation agencies. RE-RMI helps uncover existing barriers, guiding stakeholders toward effective, targeted actions for accelerating the shift to a low-carbon future.

Based on survey insights, the tool also generates recommendations tailored to regional contexts, incite authorities to implement context specific interventions and foster sustainable energy solutions at the grassroots level.
